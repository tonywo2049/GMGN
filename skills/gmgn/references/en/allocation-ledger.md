中文版本：[../zh-CN/allocation-ledger.md](../zh-CN/allocation-ledger.md)

New projects do not use this template. Use it only for a cross-milestone requirement pool
that predates GMGN, then archive it. New ideas enter the ROADMAP TODO list.

```markdown
| legacy id | source | summary | target milestone | target requirement | rationale | status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| L-1 | <path:line> | <original intent> | M1 | R1 or pending | <why> | allocated |
```

Reconcile the totals: source inventory = allocated + explicitly rejected + pending decision.
No item may disappear silently.
